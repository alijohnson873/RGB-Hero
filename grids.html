RGB HERO

Instructions

  • You are every odd circle. The computer is every even circle.
  • You have 20 seconds to change your RGB values to match the color of the computers circles.
  • The closer you are to a color match, your cicles will turn into squares.

Controls

  • Tap 'A', 'W' and 'D' to switch between controlling red, green and blue values.
  • Hold'Up' and 'down' keys to increase or decrease the RGB value.
