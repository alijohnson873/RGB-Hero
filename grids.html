RGB HERO

Instructions

  • The task of the game is to match the colors in the circles within 20 seconds.
  • The color of every even circle is fixed but you can control the RGB values of every odd circle using the controls (see right).
  • The closer the colors are, the lower the color difference value will be. A color difference value of 0 is a perfect match.

Controls

  • Tap A, W or D to switch between controlling RED, GREEN and BLUE values.
  • Hold UP and DOWN keys to increase or decrease the RGB value.

High Score:

20s
